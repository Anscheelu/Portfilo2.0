Scanning the unscannable

Modul

3D Design

Media

Blender, Scaniverse

Lecturer

Tena Keleman

Team

Taylor Kovacevic

In this research project, we explored the possibilities of 3D scanners and how they perceive reflection, depth, and space. Since traditional scanners can't interpret reflections, we worked with Scanniverse, which uses LiDAR sensors. The outcome is a fascinating exploration of how reflections and mirrors are interpreted as depth, resulting in a new, imaginary space.
