Point of View

Modul

Typography II

Media

Staging in space, Adobe Premiere Pro

Lecturer

Jinsu Ahn

Team

Ayleen Weidmann

Through the phrase "point of view," we created a projection that changes its readability with perspective. The goal was to integrate the meaning of the phrase into our installation. It was a great interplay between perspectives, space, and typography. The staging project challenged us to think about how a two-dimensional composition is interpreted in three-dimensional space. We learned more about how we perceive space, perspectives, and projections.
