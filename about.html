About

Get to know me

My name is Angela and I‘m studying visual communication and digital spaces in the Bachelor program. I am constantly exploring new mediums and ideas that challenge the boundaries of art and technology.

My style

My style is a mix of structure and experimentation. I’m always curious and love exploring different media, forms and compositions. I enjoy pushing ideas to find the perfect balance between meaning and design.

My skills

During my studies, I improved my skills and learned to use different programs. Whether it’s video editing, photography, scenography, 3D design or coding I‘m always open to enrich my skills in any kind of field.

My interests

Next to my studies I like to paint, cook and take pictures of my surroundings. On weekends I work as a mathematic tutor for kids and adults but mostly on highschool level.
