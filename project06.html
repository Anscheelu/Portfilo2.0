Kontrast

Media

Processing

In this open task about contrasts, I decided to create an interactive script in Processing. The game is a product that combines different contrasts. Within the design, I play with the black-and-white contrast. The letters and words contrast between ordered/unordered, readable/unreadable, and static/dynamic. For the typography, I chose to work with lines only. The letters are calculated and generated in Processing.
